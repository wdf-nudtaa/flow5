The rotation are performed around 
\begin{itemize}
	\item the $y$-axis for the angle of attack $\alpha$
	\item the $z_w$-axis for the angle of attack $\beta$
\end{itemize}

$$\begin{matrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
x  \\
y  \\
z 
\end{bmatrix}_{wind} =
\begin{bmatrix}
\cos{\beta}\cos{\alpha}                    &\sin{\beta}         &\cos{\beta}\sin{\alpha}  \\
-\sin{\beta}\cos{\alpha}                   &\cos{\beta}         &-\sin{\beta}\sin{\alpha}  \\
-\sin{\alpha}                          &0               &\cos{\alpha}
\end{bmatrix} 
\end{matrix} 
\begin{bmatrix}
X  \\
Y  \\
Z 
\end{bmatrix}$$

$$\begin{matrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
X  \\
Y  \\
Z 
\end{bmatrix} =
\begin{bmatrix}
\cos{\beta}\cos{\alpha}             &-\sin{\beta}\cos{\alpha}         &-\sin{\alpha}  \\
\sin{\beta}                   &\cos{\beta}                 &0  \\
\cos{\beta} \sin{\alpha}              &-\sin{\beta}\sin{\alpha}         &\cos{\alpha}
\end{bmatrix} 
\end{matrix} 
\begin{bmatrix}
x  \\
y  \\
z 
\end{bmatrix}_{wind}$$
